Frequently Asked Questions

Do you book the flights for me?

No. We operate purely as an advisory service to protect your account security. We find the exact flights and provide you with a foolproof "Booking Manual." You will log into your own accounts, transfer the points, and book the ticket yourself using our instructions.

What if the award space disappears?

Award space is highly dynamic and can vanish in minutes. This is why we require you to be ready to book immediately upon receiving your manual. If the space disappears before you transfer your points, we will attempt to find an alternate route or refund the fee. However, once you transfer points, we cannot control airline inventory.

What if I don't have enough points?

If your points balance is too low for your desired route, we will let you know upfront during the initial inquiry stage and will not proceed or charge you.

Why a 10% fee?

Flat fees incentivize consultants to do the bare minimum. A percentage-based success fee aligns our interests with yours: we are financially motivated to find you the absolute cheapest points redemption compared to the highest cash price.

Ready to clear your points balance?

No upfront commitment. We only charge if we find a high-value redemption.
